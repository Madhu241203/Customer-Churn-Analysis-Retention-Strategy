# Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy

## Project Overview

Customer churn is a critical business challenge that directly impacts revenue, profitability, and customer lifetime value. This project aims to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, determine the root causes of customer attrition, and recommend effective retention strategies using Business Analysis methodologies and Data Analytics tools.

The project combines Business Analysis documentation with data-driven insights generated through Python, MySQL, and Power BI to support proactive decision-making and improve customer retention.

---

# Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services. Existing processes are typically reactive, resulting in delayed interventions, reduced customer satisfaction, and significant revenue loss.

The absence of centralized churn monitoring, customer segmentation, and predictive insights limits the organization's ability to retain valuable customers and make informed strategic decisions.

---

# Project Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior and trends.
* Identify key factors contributing to customer attrition.
* Measure the business impact of customer churn.
* Develop customer segmentation for risk identification.
* Create interactive dashboards for churn monitoring.
* Recommend retention strategies to reduce customer loss.
* Enable data-driven decision-making.

# Business Analysis Deliverables

The project follows the complete Business Analysis lifecycle:

### 1. Business Requirement Document (BRD)

Defines the business problem, objectives, project scope, stakeholders, and expected outcomes.

### 2. Stakeholder Matrix

Identifies project stakeholders, their roles, responsibilities, influence levels, and communication requirements.

### 3. Functional Requirement Document (FRD)

Documents detailed functional requirements necessary to achieve business objectives.

### 4. User Stories

Captures user needs and expectations from the proposed solution.

### 5. Process Analysis Document

Analyzes the current business process and identifies inefficiencies.

### 6. Gap Analysis

Compares the current state (AS-IS) with the future state (TO-BE) and identifies improvement opportunities.

### 7. Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)

Ensures all requirements are mapped to corresponding testing activities.

### 8. User Acceptance Testing (UAT)

Validates that the final solution satisfies business requirements and stakeholder expectations.

# Key Findings

* Customers with shorter tenure exhibit higher churn rates.
* Month-to-month contracts show greater churn compared to long-term contracts.
* Customer satisfaction strongly influences retention.
* Revenue loss is concentrated within specific customer segments.
* Lack of proactive monitoring contributes to increased customer attrition.
